Hi

My name is Ulises!

I am a multidisciplinary designer with over 10 years of combined experience in branding, graphic design, architectural design, and user experience design.

I hold a Master’s degree in Architectuarl Design from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, where I was recipient of the ‘MIT Architecture Graduate Student Fellowship Award’.

Before the end of my last semester at MIT, I joined enterprise software startup Smarking as UX/UI lead, establishing the brand of the company while refreshing the interface of a data analytics tool used by select garage managers in the United States. When working with Smarking, we were accepted into the Winter 2015 round of Y-Combinator, and secured a seed round of $3MM USD shortly after.

After my time with Smarking, I moved to New York to become Head of Design at the fast growing ad-tech startup, AdHawk. AdHawk (Techstars Boulder 2015) is a mobile app that aggregates a company's digital marketing data, and offers them one-click optimization solutions to improve their campaigns' performance.

I have a passion for working with startups and providing them with the vision, brand identity, and user experience design that positions them for optimal growth.
